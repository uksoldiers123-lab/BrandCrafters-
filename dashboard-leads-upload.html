Leads & CSV Upload

Upload CSV lead lists into call_leads so contractors can work them in the portal.

1. Prepare your CSV

Suggested columns: business_name, phone, alt_phone, email, alt_email, city, state, website, social, priority, call_status, assigned_caller_email, notes

First row is headers. Each row becomes a record in call_leads as long as those columns exist in Supabase.
