Scripts & WebBook

Use this as your cheat sheet when you call, email, or DM new businesses about getting a website built by BrandCrafters.

How Commissions Work

Important You only contact new clients and get paid for new website package sales.

  • Starter Craft (1-page) — Commission: $50
  • Business Builder (3–5 pages) — Commission: $75
  • E-Commerce Store — Commission: $100

No commission is paid on small add-ons or maintenance by themselves — those only happen later with existing clients. Your focus is simple: book a new website sale, get paid on that package.

Commissions are tracked in our system when a lead is marked Closed Won and the package is selected.

Pricing · Quick Reference

Core Website Packages

  • Starter Craft — $249
    1-page site (Home • About • Contact), basic logo, domain + SSL, mobile-ready.
  • Business Builder — $699
    3–5 page business website with services, photos, and contact.
  • E-Commerce Store — $1,199
    Online shop or booking site with checkout.

Best fits (examples):

  • Starter: solo barbers, lawn care, side hustle services.

  • Maintenance Plans (monthly)

These are useful to mention but they are not required to close a website sale. Your job is to get them comfortable saying “yes” to a package (Starter / Business / E-Com).

Phone Call Scripts

Adjust for the type of business (restaurant, lawn care, trucking, salon, etc.). Don’t read these word-for-word; use them as a guide.

1. Cold Call — No Website (Priority A)

Hi, is this {{BusinessName}}?

Hey {{OwnerName}}, my name is {{YourName}}. I’m calling with BrandCrafters — we build simple, done-for-you business websites.

I was looking up {{BusinessName}} and I noticed you don’t really have a full website yet — people mainly see a listing or social page. 
Most of the businesses we help were in the same spot: they depend on calls and word of mouth, but they don’t have a site that actually shows what they do.

Quick question:
If someone searches your business tonight, are you happy with what they see?

[Let them respond]

The reason I’m calling is we offer:
• A basic starter site at $249  
• A full 3–5 page business site at $699  

We handle the domain, setup, and design — you just approve it.

Would you be open to a quick follow-up where I text or email you a simple breakdown and a couple examples, then you can decide if it’s worth it?

2. Cold Call — Weak Site / Only Facebook (Priority B)

Hi, is this {{BusinessName}}?

Hey {{OwnerName}}, this is {{YourName}} with BrandCrafters. 
I found you on {{Facebook/Google/etc.}} and I like what you’re doing — but I noticed your main online presence is {{a basic page / an older site / just Facebook}}.

Right now, when people look you up, they mainly see {{what you have now}}.
Most businesses we help were in the same spot — they look smaller or less trustworthy online than they really are in real life.

We build modern sites starting at $249, and our most popular package is a 3–5 page business site at $699 that looks good on phones, lists your services, and makes it easy to contact you.

If I send you a quick breakdown with examples by text or email, will you at least look at it and tell me what you think?

3. Closing / Next Step

Awesome. The next step is simple:

1. I’ll send you a short breakdown of our packages (Starter, Business, or E-Com).
2. You pick what fits best.
3. We handle the setup and design — you just approve and send your info/photos.

What’s the best email and cell number to send that to?
