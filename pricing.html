How BrandCrafters compares.

There are a lot of ways to get a website. Here’s where we sit compared to DIY builders, cheap freelancers, and traditional agencies.

DIY Builders (Wix, Squarespace)

  • $200–$300/year in subscription costs
  • You design and build everything yourself
  • No custom support or strategy
  • No business email included
  • Templates that look like everyone else’s

Good if you enjoy tech and have time. Not ideal if you just want a finished site.

Cheap Freelancers / Fiverr

  • Advertised as $50–$200, but upsell for each feature
  • Often no domain, hosting, or email included
  • Communication can be inconsistent
  • Quality and reliability vary a lot

Can work, but often costs more time and stress than it’s worth.

Local Web Designers

  • Usually $700–$2,000+ for a basic site
  • Domain & email may be separate line items
  • Revisions and meetings can drag timelines out
  • Design quality depends heavily on who you find

Some are great partners. Others aren’t. It’s hit or miss.

Stripe payment setup (optional add-on).

If you’d like customers to pay you online, we offer Stripe setup as an add-on. You choose whether you want to complete onboarding yourself or have us handle it for you.

Option 1 — You complete onboarding

  • We send you Stripe’s secure onboarding link
  • You enter your own ID, SSN/EIN, and bank information
  • Best if you prefer full control of sensitive data

After approval, we plug your Stripe Payment Links into your website.

Option 2 — We handle setup

  • You fill out a Stripe Setup Questionnaire after purchase
  • We complete the Stripe setup process for your business
  • We create your first payment links in Stripe
  • We add “Pay Now / Book Now” buttons to your site

Best if you want a done-for-you setup with minimal tech steps.
